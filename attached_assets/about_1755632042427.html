​​TSSA Certified Contractor
​
The Technical Standards & Safety Authority (TSSA) enhances public safety through inspections, engineering reviews, training, and outreach. As a self-funded, not-for-profit, TSSA provides cost-effective safety solutions.
For safe operation of your natural gas or propane furnaces:
✅ Annual Inspections: Have your furnace inspected yearly by a qualified professional.
✅ Certified Contractors: Use only TSSA-registered heating contractors. Verify status at (416) 734-3300 or 1-877-682-TSSA (8772).
✅ Verify Identity: Always request identification to avoid unsolicited offers.
✅ Multiple Estimates: Obtain at least three written estimates outlining the scope of work, contractor details, timelines, and any warranty or maintenance coverage.
